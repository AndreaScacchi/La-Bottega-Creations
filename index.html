About Us

Arte è quando la mano, la testa, e il cuore dell’uomo vanno insieme.

Esplora la nostra realtà

Every woman has a unique story to tell. We hope our creations will inspire you to share yours with the world.



WELCOME to La Bottega Creations, an artisanal shop in the heart of Italy where we create handmade and unique jewels. We craft earrings, rings, bracelets, pendants, and necklaces. Every woman has a unique story to tell, and we hope our creations will inspire you to share yours with the world. Find out more about our collection here on La Bottega Creations, on Etsy and on Facebook.
